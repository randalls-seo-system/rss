# Article Structure — Comparison (X vs Y)

## ATF Section
Same as guide structure. Quick cards should highlight the key differences.

## Main Content

```
<div class="vlnPage main-content">

  H2: The Bottom Line Up Front
  - Bold verdict: which option wins for most readers and why

  H2: [Option A] vs [Option B] at a Glance
  - Comparison table (vlnTableScroll + vlnTable)
  - Columns: Feature | Option A | Option B

  H2: When [Option A] Is the Better Choice
  - Specific scenarios, numbers, thresholds
  - Callout with key decision factor

  H2: When [Option B] Is the Better Choice
  - Same depth as Option A section
  - Callout with key decision factor

  H2: [Cost/Financial Comparison]
  - Table with dollar amounts, rates, fees
  - CTA pill

  H2: [Common Mistakes / What Most People Get Wrong]
  - Practical warnings
  - Callout

  H2: The Bottom Line
  H2: Frequently Asked Questions
  H2: Resources Used

</div>
```

## Word Count Target: 1,800-2,500 words
## Required: At least 2 comparison tables
